Background

At first, it seemed like a distant concept, but my advisor's urgency was spot on. Learning machine learning was no small feat, but I took the challenge head-on. I delved deep into the world of deep learning, took courses in machine learning, and went for a PhD in the topic. But my journey isn't over yet. There's so much more to explore, and I'm excited to be a part of it!
