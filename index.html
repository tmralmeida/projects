Background

During my Master Thesis I studied Computer Vision methods for detecting road lanes/lines onboard of an autonomous vehicle. Deep Learning emerged right from the start as one of the most viable/robust solutions to this problem. But I had only heard about this topic in the halls... My advisor told me that there were deadlines to meet and I would need a lot of time to learn Machine Learning (barely I knew how right he was). The rest is history: I started there right away studying the high level of Deep Learning; when I finished my thesis I took several courses about both Machine Learning and Deep Learning topics and today I am on the right track. However, there is still a world to discover and explore and I am going to be a part of it!
